Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 12, 2025

1. Overview

Schumann Field values your privacy. This app does not collect, transmit, or share personal data with any third parties. All data related to HealthKit, heart rate, or sleep metrics stays securely on your device.

2. HealthKit

If you grant access, Schumann Field reads certain HealthKit data (sleep analysis, heart rate, HRV) for visualization and analysis within the app. No HealthKit data leaves your device. We do not write or modify HealthKit data.

3. Analytics and Tracking

Schumann Field contains no advertising, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking.

4. Subscriptions

Subscriptions and purchases are handled securely by Apple’s in-app purchase system. We do not store your payment information.

5. Contact

For any privacy questions, contact us at privacy@schumannfield.com.
